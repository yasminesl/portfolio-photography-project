About

Yasmine Slavenburg

I am a Charlotte based hobby photographer, originally from the Netherlands. Through photography, I wanted to share my creativity and love for the beauty I find in many things around me. I think pictures tell a thousand words as well as show what the perspective of the photographer is.

After completing my B.S. in Psychology, I decided to learn how to code. With the skills that I achieved I was able to create this photography portfolio to showcase my work and what I am passionate about. I have always had a love for photography, taking pictures when I was younger.
